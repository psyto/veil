## Best Practices

1. **Tag strategically** - Don't over-tag, but include @QuickNode when relevant
2. **Show, don't tell** - Screenshots, demos, code snippets
3. **Be genuine** - They can spot forced engagement
4. **Provide value** - Offer case study, feedback, content
5. **Time it right** - Tweet when their team is active (check their timezone)
6. **Engage with their content** - Reply to their tweets meaningfully
